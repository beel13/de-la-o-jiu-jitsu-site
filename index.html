Fight nights live here.

Two stories, a ring, mats, and a community that shows up. This is a fight house, not a fitness franchise.

05 / Before your first class

Straight answers.

I'm brand new. Is this place too hardcore for me?

No. The same gym that trains pro fighters is described in reviews as safe, friendly, and family. First day students are normal here, and the room looks after its own.

Which program should I start with?

Depends what you want: grappling and self defense, start BJJ. Striking, start Muay Thai or boxing. Not sure? Text us and John will point you to the right first class.

How old do kids need to be?

The kids self defense program runs Tuesday and Thursday evenings for boys and girls. Text us with your kid's age and we will confirm placement.

Do I need a gi or gear to start?

Just workout clothes and water for your first session. Gear comes later.

What does it cost?

Come in, try it, and talk to John about a plan that fits. No pressure at the door.

When can I just come watch or roll?

Friday open mat at 5 pm is the easiest first look. Come watch a round, meet the room, and see if it fits.
